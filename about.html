About Dixit Group

  1. Home
  2. About

The Dixit Group is a consortium of companies that are leaders in providing business in Gold,Silver,Sculpture,Music,Technology. It is always our honour to serve our customers and we are always ready to Deliver the best products to our customers. Customer satisfaction is our main motto.To provide best products to our customers and always keep them happy. To provide maximum services under one organization.

The Dixit Group Of Companies was started with a purpose to bring all our business under on hood. we have always worked hard for our customers and want them to know that we are much more than one organization. We are always happy to serve our customers, through Dixit Group Of Companies we will be providing them our services in the field of Gold,Silver,Sculptures,Music,hardware and Software technology.

The Dixit Group Of Companies works in five different fields namely Gold,Silver,Sculptures ,Music,Hardware and software technologies.We have professional and most experience members in our consortium. Each company in this consortium is owned by our hardworking and ingenious Members.
